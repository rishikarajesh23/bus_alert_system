Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology (RSET)

Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology (RSET) is a premier technical institution known for its academic excellence and holistic development approach. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the field of engineering and technology. The college is committed to providing high-quality education and fostering a culture of innovation and creativity among its students.

College Bus Facility

The college provides efficient transport facilities to a number of cities and towns nearby. Around 20 buses are arranged for the comfortable transportation of both staff and students to multiple routes. All the buses reach the campus on or before 8:15 AM and leave by 4:45 PM every day. Students are to keep their bus cards/ID cards with them every day and are liable to produce them at any time upon request by the bus coordinator.
